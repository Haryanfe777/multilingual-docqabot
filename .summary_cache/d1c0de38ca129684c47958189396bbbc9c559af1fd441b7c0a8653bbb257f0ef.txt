The text outlines the terms of an internship agreement. The intern's weekly hours at the host organization are to be determined, and any requirements for night, Sunday, or holiday work must be specified. The intern will be supervised by an academic advisor and the host organization's designated internship supervisor, who will ensure optimal conditions for the internship. The intern is allowed to return to their educational institution for necessary courses or meetings, and the host organization may allow the intern to travel. Any issues during the internship must be reported to the academic advisor and educational institution for resolution. The intern may receive a stipend according to the host country's laws.
