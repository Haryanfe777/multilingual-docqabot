The text is a template for an internship completion certificate, which includes fields for the student's personal information, the title of the course or curriculum they are following, the name of the educational institution or training organization, and the duration of the internship. The total duration of the internship is calculated based on the actual presence of the student in the organization, with 7 hours of presence considered equivalent to one day, and 22 days equivalent to one month. The intern receives a stipend for the internship, the amount of which is included in the certificate. The certificate is crucial for considering the internship work for retirement benefits, subject to a fee.
