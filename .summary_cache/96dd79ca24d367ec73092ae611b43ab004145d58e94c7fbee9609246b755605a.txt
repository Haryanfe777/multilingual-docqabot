Chapter 3 of TNCD discusses non-metastatic colon cancer in patients under 40 years old. Testing for MSI or dMMR is recommended to identify Lynch syndrome. Familial polyposis should be considered if more than 10 colorectal adenomatous polyps are present, with genetic testing recommended for APC and MUTYH mutations. Other rare genes may also be associated with hereditary colorectal polyposis.
