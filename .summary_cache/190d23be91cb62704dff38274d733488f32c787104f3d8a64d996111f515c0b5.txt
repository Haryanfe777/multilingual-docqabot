Various guidelines and studies have been conducted on post-polypectomy colonoscopy surveillance, physical activity and body size on survival after colorectal cancer diagnosis, genotype-guided dose individualization of fluoropyrimidine therapy, adjuvant fluorouracil and folinic acid in colon cancer, duration of adjuvant oxaliplatin-fluoropyrimidine combination therapy, follow-up strategies for non-metastatic colorectal cancer patients, and advanced imaging for detection of colorectal neoplasia.
