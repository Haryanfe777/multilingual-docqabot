CAPOX administered for 3 months is more effective than FOLFOX6m for colon cancer treatment. Oxaliplatin should be stopped if neuropathy of grade 2 or higher occurs. DPD deficiency should be tested for and doses adjusted accordingly. For patients over 70, mono-chemotherapy with fluoropyrimidine for 6 months is recommended. Adjuvant chemotherapy with fluoropyrimidines and oxaliplatin is beneficial for stage III colon cancer, regardless of tumor status. For patients unable to tolerate oxaliplatin, alternative treatments include 5-FU with folinic acid or oral capecitabine for 6 months.
