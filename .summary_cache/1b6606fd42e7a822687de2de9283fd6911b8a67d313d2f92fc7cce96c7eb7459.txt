The text is a French legal document related to an internship agreement. It is governed by French law and any unresolved disputes will be subject to French jurisdiction. The agreement requires signatures from representatives of the educational institution, the host organization, the intern (and their legal representative if applicable), the intern's teacher, and the internship tutor. The university reserves the right not to sign the agreement if it is submitted after the internship has started. An internship certificate is to be issued to the intern upon completion of the internship.
