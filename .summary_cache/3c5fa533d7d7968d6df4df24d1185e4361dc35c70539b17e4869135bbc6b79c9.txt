The intern is required to obtain a contract for assistance, including medical repatriation and legal assistance, and an individual accident insurance contract. If a vehicle is provided by the host organization, the intern must ensure it is insured for student use. If the intern uses their own vehicle, they must declare it to the insurer. If the security conditions in the host country deteriorate, the intern must inform their school and end their internship, returning to a safe country. A replacement internship may be arranged. The intern is subject to the host organization's disciplinary and regulatory terms and must adhere to schedules and health and safety regulations. Disciplinary sanctions are decided by the educational institution. The host organization can terminate the internship for serious breaches of discipline. Leave and internship interruptions follow the host organization's regulations.
